We only collect the following data that is necessary for the service to function:

Your data is used to:

3. How We Protect Data

We take security seriously. Your data is stored securely and we limit who has access to it.

4. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell your data. We only share data if required by law. We do not use any third parties to process your data.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access your data this is the right to request to see what we store about you
  • Correct data this is the right to fix errors in your information
  • Delete data this is the right to request that we delete all your information
  • Export data this is the right to get a copy of your data
  • Withdraw consent this is the right to withdraw your consent at any time

6. Data Retention

Your data is stored as long as you have an active account. Upon account deletion, all your personal data will be removed from our systems.
